This utility will calculate the # of miles needed for a Frequent Flyer award, and also help plan a OneWorld Explorer ticket
 
Jordan Hargrave originally wrote this tool, and most of the clever stuff is his doing. SLF has been maintaining it in recent times with updated flight schedules, changes to OWE rules and some feature enhancements. Please use one of the discussion threads above to provide feedback about this tool. You can also follow the latest developments of Mileage Monkey on Twitter - news will be posted there first.
